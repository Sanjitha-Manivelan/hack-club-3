Tasty treats for dogs! Dog Treats

Dog treats are small, specially made snacks given to dogs as rewards, training aids, or occasional indulgences. They come in many forms—biscuits, jerky, soft chews, or dental sticks—and are often flavored with meat, peanut butter, or other dog-safe ingredients. While treats can be a great way to reinforce good behavior and bond with your pet, they should be given in moderation to avoid weight gain or health issues. Choosing high-quality treats with natural ingredients is best, and it’s important to avoid anything containing harmful substances like chocolate, xylitol, grapes, or excess salt.
